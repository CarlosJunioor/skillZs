# skillZs — Style Guide

## Style DNA (locked, never changes)
- Circular fisheye composition, heavy black outer vignette
- Urban comic illustration, thick black ink lineart (no thin strokes)
- Grunge overlay: paper grain, scratches, dust speckle
- Muted, desaturated palette — never saturated, never pastel
- Close-up character foreground + warped interior background

## Color Palette
- Skin:        olive yellow   #C8C346 → shadow #8A8628
- Walls:       dusty mauve    #9A6E7A
- Drips/tags:  deep purple    #5C3D6B
- Floor/props: warm brown     #7C5C3E
- Whites:      bone cream     #E8DFC9   (never pure white)
- Lineart:     near-black     #1A1A1A   (never pure black)

## Character Anatomy
- Oversized head vs body (fisheye exaggeration)
- Jet-black spiky hair, jagged silhouette
- Small asymmetric eyes, tiny black pupils, lots of sclera
- Extreme wide grin, full teeth grid + gum line visible
- Bandage / scar / dirt detail on cheek
- Dark hoodie with small skull motif, drawstrings visible
- Optional: hoop earring, piercing, plaster

## Environment
- Run-down domestic interior (kitchen / bathroom / bedroom / basement)
- Old appliances, cracked tile, peeling wall texture
- Tagged walls (graffiti words tied to the project)
- Small narrative prop: cardboard box, note, sticker
- Speech bubble with a single glyph (!, ?, ♥)

## Typography (in-image text)
- Hand-tagged graffiti, drippy purple
- Use it for project names, skill names, signatures
- Small occult micro-doodles allowed (star, skull) — sparingly

## Variation Axes (safe to change per render)
- Character identity & pose
- Setting / room
- Graffiti words on the wall
- Speech bubble glyph
- Box label / note text

## Hard Don'ts
- No glossy AI-render finish, no 3D, no Pixar softness
- No pure white / pure black
- No copyrighted likenesses — original characters only
- No symmetrical fisheye — always slightly off-center

## Prompt Template
> Fisheye circular portrait of {CHARACTER}, urban comic illustration,
> thick ink lineart, grunge paper texture, {POSE/EXPRESSION},
> dark hoodie with skull detail, set in {SETTING},
> drippy purple graffiti on wall reading "{WORD}",
> speech bubble with "{GLYPH}", small {PROP} in foreground,
> muted palette: olive yellow, dusty mauve, deep purple, cream,
> heavy black vignette border, no glossy rendering.
